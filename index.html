Mubarrat Choudhury

CEO, CharityStack

Main: Product | Secondary: Design | Tertiary: Engineering

Currently, I'm the co-founder & CEO of a company called CharityStack, a pre-seed stage company that has generated more than $22 million in revenue for nonprofits, $600k in ARR and has raised $1 Million in venture capital.

Before starting my company, I worked at Apple as a Product Manager helping develop a Conversational AI product. My time there was spent working at the intersection of Siri & the Messages app for business applications.

The core function of business is to provide more time for individual leisure. However, we are an era where the vast majority of human beings are working more hours than any other generation. I believe that humans can only thrive if they are given the opportunity to replace time as function of earning money, with building assets. And this can only be done by giving everyone the opportunity to build a business for themselves. I'm on a mission to build the everything company for B2B.
